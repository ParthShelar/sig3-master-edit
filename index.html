  • ACM

    The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world’s largest educational and scientific society uniting professionals, educators and researchers in the field of computer science to inspire dialogue, share resources and address the challenges in the domain. The purpose of this organization is to advance the scientific development, construction and application of the new machinery for computing, reasoning, and handling of information. The mission is to offer the students an opportunity to learn, innovate and grow into intellectuals, visionaries and technocrats, who contribute to promoting the advancements in science and technology. The ACM India Council is an effort of ACM aimed at improving the level of ACM activities across the country. The ACM India Council comprises a cross-section of the Computer Science and Information Technology communities committed to increasing the visibility and relevance of ACM in India.

  • pulzion

    Pulzion is the annual technical fest organized by PICT ACM Student Chapter. Pulzion has hosted multiple events including coding competition ranging from amateur competitions two day-long as well as mock placements, business management based and quizzing events. It has become one of the most anticipated events taking place at PICT with participants from colleges all over Pune. With high aspirations, backed with sincerity and dedication, the PASC team aims to add value to the college and all the people in it.

  • ACM-W

    Association for Computing Machinery's Council on Women in Computing is an exclusive woman's only wing of ACM which supports & commemorates the participation, contribution and achievements of women in the technical domain. Encouraging gender equality and women empowerment, ACM W promotes full engagement of women in the technical domain. Through its collaborations and initiatives, ACM W provides a large range of services to all ACM members along with pushing the advancement of contribution of women in the field of computing.

  • Celebration of Women in Computing

    PICT ACM Student Chapter (PASC), in collaboration with ACM-W India & ACM Pune Professional Chapter, organized an one of a kind event in: “Celebration of Women in Computing” on 24 February 2018 to encourage full engagement of women in the computing field as well as highlight the achievements of the female leading lights in the Tech industry and academia. Dr.. Indira Parik and Dr.. Swati Sardesai, graced the event with their presence as chief guests, along with Dr.. Arati Dixit, Mrs. Shubhangi Kale & Mrs.Sahilaja Gaur, as guests of honour. The event provided a platform for aspiring female technocrats to meet & interact with pioneers in industry & academia. The guests, through their speeches and talks, made the audience aware of various challenges they faced and how they overcame them, which left one & all present awe stricken. The talk by Mrs. Pranjal Gundesha was particularly captivating and inspiring. A panel discussion on “Career options for Women in Computing” highlighted various career options in the technical domain & its viability for women. It was an enlightening experience for the audience. The interactive meet & discussion was followed by a number of fun events & competitions such as Just Coding, Tech MCQ Quiz and more which further captivated the audience.

  • Benefits of Membership

    Individual ACM members are referred to as ACM Chapter Member. ACM chapter member enjoy a host of benefits such as 3-month complimentary ACM communication subscription, full year free subscription to ​XDRS​ , the ACM student magazine along with an ​acm.org​ email ID and more. The members gain valuable exposure and experience by interacting with over a hundred thousand fellow members and peers. Apart from that, members are also eligible to avail subscription of ACM digital library, use facilities of ACM Career & Job Centre and receive various popular ACM newsletters.Such benefits & privileges aim to support & assist the member to expand their academic & industrial acumen in the field of computing which goes a long way in advancing their professional career and interests in the domain of technology & computing.
